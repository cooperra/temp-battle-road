Battle Road is an action adventure comic about a world full of magical, battle-equipped musical instruments and a guy with a ‘fro named Fro who uses his keytar to take on Battle Road: The road that leads through all of the best venues and all of the biggest labels around. Only the toughest of the battle musicians make it through. The comic updates with two new pages every Thursday.



I’m Darren, a cartoonist from Louisville, Kentucky. I’ve drawn comic strips for my other website, Merlin Comics for nearly seven years, and I’ve been drawing for as long as I can remember. I’m heavily influenced by the work of Eiichiro Oda (One Piece) and Bryan Lee O’Malley (Scott Pilgrim) just to name two. I moonlight as a giant turtle monster that destroys small towns.
